I’ve recently launched a new landing page for my consultation service, where you can book a quick session with me to discuss the tasks you want to automate. This is a pilot session, and the goal is to help me shape the format of my offerings.

Why Automation?

I’ve always wanted to build a service or product that provides positive value. One of the values that resonates most with me is nurturing the skill of selectively automating tasks because not all tasks are worth automating.

My first job in data entry required me to edit, convert, and upload hundreds of digital magazines every week. While I could have written scripts to handle those jobs, my programming skills were limited at the time. Discovering tools like Keyboard Maestro allowed me to achieve the same goal through a user-friendly interface.

That’s when I learned to be ten times more productive in data entry.

What are the positive benefits of being more productive and efficient in my work?

  • I can finish my job faster and focus on the quality of the magazines.
  • I create more time for myself to learn new skills.
  • I reduce the risk of wrist injuries since repetitive motions can be executed with a single keystroke.
  • I remove the mental weight of dealing with cumbersome tasks, allowing me to easily enter a focused zone.

Doing Less for More

If you want to perform at a higher level, you need to balance thinking and doing. By delegating tasks you don’t find enjoyable, you can free up more time for the things that matter to you. For me, it’s spending time with my wife and driving my kids to school every day.

For some professionals, it can mean setting up your workspace for different contexts. You can have a macro built to switch between meetings, writing, and design sessions.

With enough creativity, you can also build many utilities to help you become more productive at your job. For example, I canceled my TextExpander subscription and built a Keyboard Maestro macro that does the same thing.

What to Expect from the Session

The pilot session will be free, and the focus will be on learning what you want to automate. We will also discuss whether it’s necessary to automate with macOS or explore other web products that might do the job better. I would love to chat with you if you’re interested in creating more value for yourself.

The pilot sessions will be available for only 5 people. If you’re interested, you can check out my calendar and pick a time that suits you.
