Quadrant Matrix Templates

Ownership Matrix

Introduced in The Focus Course by Shawn Blanc, you want to place your tasks and activities on one of the quadrants.

The goal from completing this matrix is to help you figure out what's taking up your schedule and identify the busy work.

You LOVE Doing This
You DISLIKE Doing This
ANYBODY Can Do This
ONLY YOU Can Do This
